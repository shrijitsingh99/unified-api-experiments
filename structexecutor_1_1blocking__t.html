Detailed Description

A behavioral property that guarantees executors provide about the blocking behavior of their execution functions.

Provides 3 nested property which represent different blocking behaviours:

  1. Possible - May or may not block depending on context no guarantees are made
  2. Always - Always blocking
  3. Never - Never blocking

Part of Proposal P0443R13 (2.2.12.1)
